I created the website you're on from scratch! Check out the Github link to see how I did it!

On the right are mockups from when I was initially planning my portfolio.

As you can tell, my portfolio doesn't really look like any of those mockups at this point. Here are some of the reasons why.

- I wanted a more unique version of a navigation bar for my portfolio instead of the basic top-right aligned because I knew I could be more creative here with my design

- I've had iterations of my portfolio that have been more 'out there' in terms of color and being more maximalist but I slowly transitioned to a minimalist, clean design because of personal preference and changes in style through the years

- I wanted all the names of my projects to be visible at all times instead of having a drop down navigation so I could

- Didn't want a scrollable home page so visitors could navigate directly to somewhere in my navigation instead of scrolling down and getting distracted

- Wanted to be able to play around with animations and pops of colors so I wanted a simple-looking website that I could experiment with
