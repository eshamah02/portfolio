SmartDose

SmartDose integrates an intuitive mobile app and physical dispenser to aid individuals with cognitive disabilities in medication management. SmartDose includes a 3D printed physical dispenser, equipped with an Arduino microcontroller and connects to the SmartDose mobile app via HTTP requests. The mobile app allows users to easily add, edit, and delete medications, as well as log when they've refilled the dispenser. Users are able to keep track of previous, current, and future medication schedules.
